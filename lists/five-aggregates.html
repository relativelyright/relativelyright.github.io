The Five Aggregates of Clingning

upādāna khanda

Alternatively: The Five Clinging-Aggregates

# Pali Translation
1 rūpūpadanākkhando the form clinging-aggregate
2 vedanūpadanākkhando the feeling clinging-aggregate
3 saññūpadanākkhando the perception clinging-aggregate
4 saṅkhārūpadanākkhando the fabrication clinging-aggregate1

the volitional formation clinging-aggregate2
5 viññāṇūpadanākkhando the consciousness clinging-aggregate

The aggregates in the The Five Aggregates are identical to the aggregates in The Five Aggregates of Clinging; the difference is that the latter denote the process of clinging to them.

Distinguishing the clinging to the aggregates with the aggregates themselves is important, because the aggregates themselves are not the problem — it is the clinging to them that is the problem.
